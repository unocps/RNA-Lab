# Dr. Pei-Chi Huang

**Associate Professor**  
Department of Computer Science  
University of Nebraska at Omaha  

Dr. Pei-Chi Huang directs the **Robotics, Networking, and Autonomous Intelligence (RNA) Laboratory**. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of Professor Aloysius K. Mok.

Her research interest lies at the intersection of intelligent robotics, cyber-physical systems (CPS), and adaptive networking. Her work specializes in enabling resource-constrained physical agents—such as humanoid robots and autonomous vehicles—to perceive, learn, and coordinate reliably in dynamic environments.

### Academic Background
* **Ph.D. in Computer Science** — University of Texas at Austin
* **Research Focus:** Real-time systems, reinforcement learning, humanoid robot communication, and digital twins.
