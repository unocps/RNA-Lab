# Join the RNA Lab

We are looking for self-driven, technically strong **M.S. and Ph.D. students** to join our team. If you are passionate about deploying AI on real hardware and solving complex networking problems, we want to hear from you.

### Preferred Technical Background
* **Languages:** Python (PyTorch/TensorFlow), C++ (highly preferred for robotics)
* **Frameworks & Tools:** ROS/ROS2, Linux/Unix environments, Git
* **Core Domains:** Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, Wireless Networking, or Real-Time Embedded Systems

### How to Apply
To apply, please send an email to **phuang@unomaha.edu** with the subject line: `[Prospective Student] Your Name - MS/PhD Application`.

Please attach:
1. Your **CV** (highlighting coding/robotics projects or publications).
2. Your **academic transcripts**.
3. A brief **research statement** detailing which RNA Lab projects interest you and why.
